In order to build this solution you will have to add references to 4 Tridion DLLs

-Tridion.Common.dll
-Tridion.ContentManager.dll
-Tridion.ContentManager.Publishing.dll
-Tridion.Logging.dll

These can be found in the {TRIDION_HOME}/bin/client directory on your content management server.
